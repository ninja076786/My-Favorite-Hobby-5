My Favorite Hobby Is:
Reading Books!

My hobby is reading any books at my freetime. I find reading books very intresting, like each time I open a new door to another world. It satisfies my curiosities, makes me forget my troubles and is a great way to pass time. Reading has many benefits; improve memory, empathy, and increase brain power. Most importantly, books give me new insights and perspectives of things that I have never learned before. Books have many diffrent categories depending on each person's intrests. For example fiction, nonfiction, realistic fiction, etc. My favorite books are Dog Man, Harry Potter, Big Nate, and Diary Of An 8-Bit Warrior. I like to read these series because they have great illustrations and a good sense of humor. Books are very convinient to bring along when you are bored. Just find a book, find a comfy spot, and start your adventure!
